Maggie Recipe

Description

Maggie is considered one of the best fast food on the mountains areas. Maggie can be cooked within few minutes. Maggie is widely eaten by mountain trekkers.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Maggie Masala
  • Veggies as per choice

Steps

  1. Boil the water for 5-8 minute.
  2. Then put the maggie into warm water and stir it for 1 minute.
  3. Add Maggie masala to it and cook it for 5-6 minute
