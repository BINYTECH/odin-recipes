Chicken Curry

Description

How would you describe a chicken curry? A typical curry from the Indian subcontinent consists of chicken stewed in an onion- and tomato-based sauce,
flavoured with ginger, garlic, tomato puree, chilli peppers and a variety of spices, often including turmeric, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, and cardamom.

Ingredients

  • Mustard Oil
  • Salt
  • Masala
  • Garlic Paste
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Warm the mustard oil for 5-7 minute.
  2. Then put the chopped onion and cooked it for atleast 8-10 minute.
  3. Then put the garlic paste and cooked it for 9-10 minute.
  4. Add masala to it and stir for 3-4 minute and then put the mutton meat into it.
  5. Cook the meat for atleast 30 minute and then add water to it.
