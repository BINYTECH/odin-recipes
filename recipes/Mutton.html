Mutton Curry

Description

Mutton curry (also referred to as kosha mangsho, lamb curry, or goat curry) is a dish that is prepared from goat meat (or sometimes lamb meat) and vegetables.
The dish is found in different variations across all states, countries and regions of South Asia.

Ingredients

  • Mustard Oil
  • Salt
  • Masala
  • Garlic Paste
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Warm the mustard oil for 5-7 minute.
  2. Then put the chopped onion and cooked it for atleast 8-10 minute.
  3. Then put the garlic paste and cooked it for 9-10 minute.
  4. Add masala to it and stir for 3-4 minute and then put the mutton meat into it.
  5. Cook the meat for atleast 30 minute and then add water to it.
